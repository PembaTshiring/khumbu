Himalung Himal expedition 7230 m



Himlung Himal

Himlung Himal is one of the peaks visited during the autumn in Nepal. Located between the border of Nepal and Tibet, in the north of Annapurna and Manaslu majestically located above the Tibetan village of Phu. A pleasant ascent snow and ice, with a new and more secure route. 3 altitude camps in addition to the base camp are required. We have also made an advanced base camp. The access trek is done in the valley of Phu village influenced by Tibetan culture. The natural beauty of the landscape offers a unique experience. The Himlung region is quite windy and cold in autumn. For this reason, if you can, it is best to do this climb in the spring.


Himalung Himal expedition (7230 m). Location of altitude camps .

  • Base camp to 4800 m. Serves on arrival for 1 day of acclimatization.
  • The advanced base camp at 5150 m serves as a real CB . For a base camp it's a good altitude.
  • Camp 1 at 5600 m. It is necessary to walk on a rather cracked glacier. Good location for the camp site.
  • Camp 2 around 6000 m . The climb to the C2 is varied with a small passage at 60 °. The location of the camp is just off the slope on a flat.
  • Camp 3 to 6400 m
  • Summit at 7230 m superb views of Annapurna, Dhaulagiri and Tibet and Mustang plateaus.
