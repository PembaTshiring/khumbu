Thapa Peak 6100 m with Dhaulagiri Tower



Trekking tour of Dhaulagiri and ascent of Thapa Peak (6100 m)

The ascent of Thapa peak or Dhampus Peak is done during the trek of the Dhaulagiri Tour. We made this climb in the last days of the trek so we are well acclimated to the altitude. No difficulty climbing the Thapa Peak from the Hidden Valley camp. We go back a moraine at the beginning not very steep the slope is accentuated a little then without exceeding the 30 Â°. Then a small not steep chimney that one team of a fixed rope makes it possible to join the final ridge. It is not a thin ridge but rather a small plateau that leads to the summit of Thapa. It's a good day because then we go down on Yak Karkha. But what a view of Nilgiri, the Tilicho. The whole trek is in tents because there is no lodge on the course.
