Ascent Saribung peak 6330 m



More than a summit a trip to another time

The Phu Mustang trek allows you to climb the Saribung peak from the Saribung pass. A trip to another time, to another world. Tibetan culture and tradition the Mustang is full of monasteries, we will visit several. What is also surprising at the start of the trek are these oases of greenery in the middle of an arid and desert landscape.
The modified route since the construction of the road between Nepal and Tibet avoids vehicles and especially dust.
To reach Lo Mantang, the capital of Mustang, the gradual climb in altitude allows a good acclimatization.
From Lo Mantang landscape change. Desert spaces as far as the eye can see, more lodge, altitudes of 5000 m with the Dhaulagiri, Annapurna or Dolpo at the bottom.
 Then the course becomes engaged especially if the bad weather is manifested.
When we walk on the glacier we go along the ice penitents on a path without problems and little steep. The altitude camps are well placed with water nearby and especially a difference in height between the small camps for a good acclimatization to the altitude.
On the day of the ascent, a very early start at 3:30 in the morning makes it possible to reach the Saribung Pass at around 6am at sunrise. The path on the glacier is not obvious at night or the interest of having a good guide who knows the course.
On the other hand, once arrived at the neck of the Saribung the summit seems very close and the course obvious. It takes approximately 2 to 3 hours to reach the summit from the pass. The ascent of the summit is optional.
Then very long descent to reach the first camp where there is water. The start of the descent on the glacial part can be tricky if there is ice and according to the state of the crevasses of the glacier. A very long day.
Return by Phu Valley and Naar Phedi with a night in the monastery.

Difficulty climbing Saribung Peak
There is no particular technical difficulty for the ascent of Saribung in good conditions. The slopes do not exceed 30 ° and are wide.
Access to the Saribung pass is from the last camp 5750 m installed on the moraine by a glacier that has a few crevasses. In addition, this access is by night so you must have a guide who knows the route. It is better to get in touch.
Finally, the return is very long because the camp is installed at the bottom of the glacier on the valley of Phu in a place where there is water.
Likewise, the descent of the glacier may need to be arranged according to the state of the glacier.
In conclusion the difficulty of this climb is especially the very big day between the departure of the camp towards 3:30 and the arrival to the following camp after having made the ascent which requires between 10:00 and 13:00 of efforts.
Finally very busy itinerary in case of bad weather especially snowfall..

In all our circuits you have a free day the day after your arrival in Nepal or the return of your trek. We offer a day of discovery of Kathmandu. Browsing Bodhnath, Pashupatinath, Bhaktapur, Patan and the heart of the old city is always a change of scenery and a wonder.
