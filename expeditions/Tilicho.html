Expedition to Tilicho Peak 7140 m





Ascent of Tilicho Peak


The Tilicho is located at the end of the Annapurna range.

We will install 2 altitude camps for the ascent. Globally until camp 2 the route follows a mixed edge clearly visible from the base camp.
CThen camp 2 at the top is a big slope with some passages at 40 Â°. Beware of wind slabs when arriving at the summit after a snowfall.
The base camp near Tilicho Lake is low around 4900 m and is very good for acclimatization to altitude.
The ascent without being technically difficult offers nice passages in mixed between the base camp and the camp 2. Then it is a race of snow to reach the summit with a superb view among others on the Annapurna. On the other hand, the difference in height is important. But exceptional view on the Mustang.

Location of the Tilicho camps:


Base camp at 4900 m
For a base camp is the ideal altitude that allows you to recover well it is on the shores of Lake Tilicho.

Camp 1 at 5600 m.
It must follow the edge clearly visible from the BC. At the top of the ridge we tilt a few meters on the other side to set up the camp.

Camp 2 to 6300 m
The climb to the C2 is a mixed route with a small passage in the rocks that we have equipped with fixed ropes. The location of the camp is just outside the ridge on a flat.

Summit at 7134 m superb views of Annapurna and the plateaus of Tibet and Mustang.
