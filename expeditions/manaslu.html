Manaslu

The Manaslu massif is located east of Annapurna. Formerly little frequented it is more today because of the difficulties of access to Tibet. Many expeditions are leaving Cho Oyu for Manaslu.

Access to the CB can be done by the first part of the trek of the Manaslu Tour to Samagaon or by the Annapurna Valley and the Larkya La pass.

In both cases the acclimatization is regular and the relatively low base camp located at 4800 m allows a good acclimatization.
The ascent does not present any particular technical difficulties other than altitude problems. 4 altitude camps are needed.
The Manaslu is also a summit that is done on skis and depending on the snow you can go down to the CB.
