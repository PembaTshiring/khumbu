Ama Dablam


Ama Dablam is located in the Khumbu Valley. Trekkers and climbers discover it on the trail that leads from Namche Bazar to Pengboche. Sometimes considered the Matterhorn of Nepal Ama Dablam is a magnificent pyramid.
From a reasonable altitude for the Himalayas the ascent of Ama Dablam is technical in a committed environment. This climb requires a good mountain experience both technical and mental.
There is also an entanglement of old fixed ropes which one must be wary of.
Ascension long forbidden Ama Dablam catches up today. There are many expeditions and you have to manage all this small world considering that the altitude camps are very limited in places.


If you can, the slower periods like spring or late November are preferable.
2014 the serac in the slope that leads to the summit threatens. Camp 3 becomes exposed and dangerous. It may be best not to camp 3 and leave Camp 2 earlier on the day of the climb. That said, well before this problem with the serac many expeditions were not camp 3 because of the limited space at this camp. Given the large number of climbers.
However, during an expedition in spring 2016 the team made camp 3 because the serac evolved "in a good way" and allowed this camp. However, there is only room for 3 - 4 tents.

Finally, 10 minutes from the base camp a recently built lodge allows wifi access.

Camps for the expedition to Ama Dablma

Base camp at 4600 m and CB advanced at 5150 m. Less than 10 minutes from the base camp a comfortable lodge offers wifi access. For small groups we suggest you to do the base camp at the lodge.
Camp 1 to 5600 m
Camp 2 at 6000 msince C1 rock climbing with passage of 5 on good granite. Gas on both sides of the ridge and arrived at the top of the Tower Jauneau is the location of C2 limited in number of places.


Camp 3 at 6300 m. Mixed ascension. Camp on the snow. Attention serac of the terminal slope becomes more and more threatening and makes dangerous the site of the camp 3. It is possible not to make camp 3 and leave very early from camp 2 to go to the top. On the other hand, big difference in altitude.
However, the serac has since evolved and in 2016 in the spring camp 3 was passable. But very little room
