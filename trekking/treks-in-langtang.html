Treks in Langtang



Langtang treks with Gosainkund lakes and Kangja La

Peaceful, relaxing, calm, serenity, contemplation, to do in slow mode

These first 2 treks (12 days and 16 days) at Langtang are an excellent first trek in Nepal.
They take place in lodges, the steps are not very long and you do not climb too high. Initially we go up a wide valley irrigated by the glaciers of Langtang with many rice fields upstairs.
Then, bypassing the sacred lakes of Gosainkung offers a panoramic view of the Himalayan range.
The Gosainkund Lakes are a place of pilgrimage for Hindus in the month of July. There are 7 lakes.
Possibility to go up to Surya Peak 5200 m.
It is necessary to count in return of the refuge Gosainkund approximately 6 hours. But what a splendid view to the top in addition to doing may be his first 5000.
The third trek to Langtang by crossing the Kangja La called also Ganja La is a more difficult route and offers a circuit little frequented far from the usual paths in an isolated and beautiful environment. Beautiful views of the peaks of Langtang 1 and 2 at 7230 m and 6580 m respectively. For this last tents are necessary to pass the La Kangja.
Established in 1976, Langtang Park is the first national park in the Himalayas. It is attached to the Gomolangma Nature Reserve of Tibet.


Highlights of treks in Langtang

  • The peaceful side of this trek.
  • The lakes Gosainkund, high place of pilgrimage, at sunset
  • Cheeses in Langtang Valley Gruyere and Mozzarella introduced by Italians
  • The lodges comfortable and flowery
  • On the trek Langtang Kangja The bivouacs under tents at the foot of the pass
  • The proximity of Kathmandu for quick access and return
