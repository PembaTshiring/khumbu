Kathmandu trekking and its valley



Visit of Kathmandu and its valley

Royal and medieval city Kathmandu does not leave indifferent

This circuit Kathmandu and its valley will take you to the heart of this fabulous city of Kathmandu and its close surroundings.
Visits to Buddhist temples, museums, spice houses, Tibetan carpet factory, with a Nepali French-speaking guide.
Then we will do a small trek of 4 days without difficulty on the hills around Kathmandu. The opportunity to stroll through the rice paddies, rubbing shoulders with the farmers in the fields and to contemplate some peaks over 8000 m of the Himalayas (Annapurna and Manaslu) at sunrise and sunset in a peaceful setting .
A dozen days to change the ideas, recharge the batteries and decompress with dates adapted to school holidays.


Services Included

  • Every night in hotel in Kathmandu with breakfasts
  • All lunch
  • 1 French Nepali guide
  • Entrances to parks, museums, etc.
  • Taxis, minibuses, etc.
  • The lodges during the little trek
  • Breakfast, lunch and dinner during the trek.
  • End of trek pot taken together
  • Hotel-airport transport A / R

Services Not Included

  • Evening meals except during the trek.
  • Personal insurance relief repatriation
  • Airport tax and visa
  • Telephone, alcoholic drinks, soda, etc.
  • The A / R France-Kathmandu plane
