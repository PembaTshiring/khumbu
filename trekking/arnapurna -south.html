Annapurna south Sanctuary and Mardi Himal





Trek Annapurna Thorong Pass

For a first trek in Nepal, one of the most beautiful gateways


Tuesday Himal as the Sanctuary of the Annapurnas are 2 short and easy treks.

For Mardi Himal when arriving at High Camp at 3600 m in the late morning those who wish can ride in the afternoon at High Camp Mardi Himal 4500 m. The view is splendid on this summit as well as on the south face of Annapurna.

At the end of the trek in the village of Lwang there is a cooperative that makes green tea that we will visit.
For the Annapurna Annapurna S trek the arrival at ABC (Annapurna Base Camp) is a magical and gigantic place with views across the entire southern face of Annapurna. Doing this trek in the spring is the opportunity to observe all along the path flowers and rhododendrons all red and pink.


Highlights of the Mardi Himal treks and Annapurna Sanctuary
  • Treks of short duration
  • Without difficulty can be done with children from 10 years
  • Comfortable lodges
  • Landscapes very diverse forests at the beginning then we will approach the high mountain
  • Views of the Annapurna, Machapuchare and Mardi Himal peaks
  • Slow acclimation to altitude.
