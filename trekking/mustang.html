Mustang Trek



Mustang by Tanggye and crossing Mustang Naar by Teri La

Buddhist monasteries and Tibetan culture immersed in a colorful landscape

The Lonely Planet guide has listed the Mustang trek as the third most beautiful tourist region in the world just behind Corsica in France and Negyam in Israel. The 2 treks of 14 and 17 days are a discovery of the kingdom of Lo and its small monasteries lost in grandiose mineral landscapes with the caves of Dhakmar. As well as a 23-day trek that connects the Mustang to the Naar valley by the Teri La at 5600 m. It is a difficult trek for experienced trekkers. Some steps are long and the passes are high. Lo Mantang the capital of Mustang is worth a visit. It is a fortified village with narrow streets that lead to places where villagers gather and women are busy at the fountain. The houses are contiguous to each other and their flat roof is characteristic of Tibetan culture. The Mustang is a desert of high altitude all in contrast of colors . Blue sky, orange wheat fields, white snowy peaks, islets of greenery, ocher rock, etc. To the ocher caves of Dhakmar legend has it that the Guru Rinpoche killed a demon and that his blood was spread on the cliffs from where this ocher-red color. Most caves are empty, but some have paintings and frescoes. Who and why we built these caves that are difficult to access in the cliff ?. It remains a mystery. Many caves date back more than 2000 years. The Mustang is isolated and the Tibetan culture very present in all the villages. The different kings of the Mustang of a dynasty more than 600 years old still take for a wife a Tibetan. Former Tibetan enclave Mustang has been in Nepal for more than 200 years. However, the king keeps some privileges for example the peasants of his kingdom cultivate his grounds. The Mustang organizes every year many Buddhist feasts. In February the ceremonies of Saka Lhuka where village chiefs are appointed. In Lo Manang the Tiji festival in May. In August the Yartung festival in Muktinath with horse races.


Highlights of Mustang treks

  • The colors of Mustang
  • The islets of greenery that contrast with the arid canyons
  • The caves of Dhakmar
  • The many monasteries
  • The Mustang festivities including the Tiji festival
  • The village of Lo Mantang capital of Mustang of beauty
  • Mustang in 14 days and Mustang by Tanggye two short treks at a reasonable altitude.
