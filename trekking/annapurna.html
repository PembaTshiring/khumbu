Arnapurna





Trek Annapurna Thorong Pass

For a first trek in Nepal, one of the most beautiful gateways


The famous Lonely Planet travel guide notes the Annapurna trekking as one of the best trekking destinations.

The trek starts at low altitude where the trail runs along the Marsyangdi river. Many rice fields, waterfalls, bamboos shape the landscape.

Then slowly we climb and the chain of Annapurnas finally appears, it is the passage of the Thorong Pass at 5300 m for the trek Annapurna Thorong Pass.
The descent on Jomson is at the door of Mustang with Buddhist and Hindu monasteries. In the end a trek that offers us a complete vision of Nepal.


The Annapurna tour is always a wonder for trekkers. Every day of walking is a different experience because of the presence of several ethnic groups and the variety of cultures.
Mythical massif linked to the ascent of Annapurna 1st summit of more than 8000 m climbed without forgetting two other summits of more than 8000 m Manaslu and Dhaulagiri that one can admire on the course.
In the afternoons we have time to visit the villages or we still walk a little to go around. We can also just rest.
For many trekkers the Annapurna Tour is often the first trek in Nepal. They are never disappointed because the landscapes are varied.


Highlights of the treks on the Annapurna Tour
  • Arrival at Thorong Pass
  • The view of the whole chain of Annapurnas side Thorong to Tilicho
  • Comfortable lodges
  • Landscapes very diverse rice fields at first, then deep gorges and peaks
  • Slow acclimation to altitude.
  • Muktinath is a pilgrimage village for Hindus and Buddhists.
