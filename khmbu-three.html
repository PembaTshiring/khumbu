Khumbu Trek Three Passes from Phaplu



Khumbu treks by Phaplu or Jiri

A range of all the Himalayas from rice fields to Everest

To reach the Khumbu valley of Everest there is another possibility than to fly to Lukla. That's what we offer with the Khumbu Three Passes trek from Phaplu.The advantage is a much better acclimatization to the altitude which will then make the most of the 3 passes that are Renjo Pass, Chola Pass and Kongma La.In addition, the whole route from Jiri or Phaplu to the path that joins Namche is very pleasant on very well maintained paths with good lodges and especially much less frequented . Less than 5% of trekkers in Khumbu leave from Phaplu or Jiri.It is also the opportunity to discover another much less tourist Khumbu more carried by agriculture, livestock and field activities. A more traditional Nepal. Sagarmatha Park in the heart of Khumbu is a World Heritage Site. It is home to a great versity of flora and fauna. This is the best way to make the Khumbu Three Passes trek or the 3 Khumbu passes and to have fun while arriving at the summit of the mountain passes and contemplating dream landscapes.From Namche the first of the 3 passes is the Renjo Pass , then after climbing to the Gokyo Peak one crosses the Chola Pass , to climb Kala Patthar and go to Everest Base Camp. We end with Kongma La .A trip to altitude and Sherpa country that starts low in the valley to acclimatize progressively and tu the peaks.The Khumbu is also many monasteries, the oldest of which is in Pangboche without forgetting to take a tour on the Tibetan market of Namche Bazar where Tibetans and Nepalese trade.


Highlights of Khumbu treks by Phaplu

  • The best acclimatization at altitude to enjoy Khumbu
  • Variety of landscapes between the lower valley and Khumbu
  • In the heart of the Himalayas of Nepal
  • Passing passes over 5000 m Renjo Pass, Chola Pass and Kongma La
  • The sunset on Everest at Kala Patthar
  • Proximity to the majestic peaks
  • Quality lodges
