Crossing Manaslu Naar Annapurna Tilicho Dhaulagiri



The great crossing of Manaslu to Dhaulagiri

The triple integral Manaslu Annapurna Dhaulagir

During this crossing Manaslu Naar Annapurna Tilicho Dhaulagiri the time slows down then stops and simple things are offered to us. This is what we learn from a long walk through different landscapes and cultures. To take back time these moments of listening, rest, silence and contemplation. This is what this trek will give you from the Tibetan valley of Manaslu, then the wild and unspoiled valley of Naar, the ascent to the desert and arid Tilicho lake to end in this valley at the end of the world the Hidden Valley during the Dhaulagiri Tower. This trek makes discover 3 peaks of more than 8000 very different m. The slender Manaslu, majestic, the chain of Annapurnas that stretches in length to Tilicho, then the Dhaulagiri with its very characteristic spur leading to the summit. During this crossing we pass the passes of the Larkya Pass at 5160 m, then the Kangla La 5300 m, the Mesokanto 5200 m and the French Pass 5200 m. A beautiful trip in altitude through regions with different cultures that can be done in both directions.
